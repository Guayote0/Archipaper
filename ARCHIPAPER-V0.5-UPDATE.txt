ARCHIPAPER v0.5 UPDATE

This cumulative update includes the v0.4 queue improvements and the new wallpaper caption feature.

What changed:

- Adds a SKIP button that keeps the image in the review queue.
- Falls back to the cached preview if a full-resolution approval download fails.
- Adds editable architect and project-name fields before approval.
- Adds a bottom-left ARCHITECT / PROJECT caption to approved online wallpapers.
- Keeps original approved image files untouched; captioned wallpaper copies are generated in Archipaper's cache.
- Fits the generated copy to each monitor so the caption remains visible.

Existing approved online images use their saved title as the project name. Their architect is shown as Unknown if that information was not recorded when they were approved.

Local-folder images are unchanged because they do not have reliable architect or project metadata.
